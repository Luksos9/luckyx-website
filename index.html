Starting in late 2025, ServiceNow made CIS-Data Foundations a mandatory prerequisite for seven CIS certifications: ITSM, Discovery, HAM, SAM, Service Mapping, SIR, and VR. If you plan to take any of those seven exams, you must pass CIS-DF first. If you already hold one of those certifications, you must pass CIS-DF by December 31, 2026 or your existing certification expires. The first CIS-DF attempt is free through June 30, 2026. After that, the exam costs $450. Seven CIS certs are not affected: CSM, HRSD, FSM, SPM, TPRM, Event Management, and GRC/IRM.
