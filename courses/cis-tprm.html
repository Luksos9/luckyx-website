4.As a final step before closing the Third-party Risk Assessment, what task(s) would the Risk Assessor typically do?
  • AFlag questions for follow up.
  • BReview comments and evaluates the risk.
  • CCreate issues within the assessment.
  • DPerform a tiering assessment.
Correct Answer: Review comments and evaluates the risk. Source ServiceNow Community: TPRM External Risk Assessments If the link fails, search Google for: ServiceNow Zurich TPRM risk assessment closing final review state Expert Explanation The TPRM assessment lifecycle moves through: Draft, Scheduled, In Progress, Awaiting Approval, and Closed. Before the Assessor advances the record to Awaiting Approval or Closed, their final task is to review all submitted questionnaire responses, read third-party comments, and evaluate the overall risk level. Intermediate steps like flagging questions or creating issues happen during the In Progress phase when reviewing individual answers.

7.When a vendor risk issue owner selects Accept during the Respond state, what happens to the control&#x27;s compliance status?
  • AThe control status changes to Accepted and is excluded from future risk calculations until the exception expires.
  • BThe control status remains non-compliant until the control is re-assessed after the exception is closed.
  • CThe control status automatically changes to Compliant when the exception is approved by the Compliance Manager.
  • DThe control status is set to Waived and the associated assessment score is recalculated excluding that control.
Correct AnswerControl status remains non-compliant until the control is re-assessed SourceServiceNow Community: TPRM Speed Learning Remediation vs acceptance ExplanationIn the Respond state, the issue owner has two paths: Remediate (fix the root cause and close the issue properly) or Accept (acknowledge the risk without fixing it). Selecting Accept creates an exception record that formally documents the known control failure and the business decision to accept the associated risk. However, the control status remains non-compliant throughout the exception period. The issue stays in the Respond state until the exception is closed.
