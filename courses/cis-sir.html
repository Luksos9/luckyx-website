  • BThreat Lookup

  • DVulnerability Scanners
0) SourceServiceNow Documentation: Threat Lookups Threat Lookup is the specific orchestration capability that queries external intelligence sources to determine the reputation of an observable.1) Correct AnswerThreat Lookup2) Detailed Expert ExplanationThreat Lookup is one of the most powerful automation features in SIR.   • CTechnique
  • DVector
